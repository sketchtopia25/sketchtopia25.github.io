Sketchtopia: A Dataset and Foundational Agents for Benchmarking Asynchronous Multimodal Communication with Iconic Feedback.

Mohd Hozaifa Khan, Ravi Kiran Sarvadevabhatla
IIIT Hyderabad
Paper (CVPR 2025) arXiv Code (GitHub) Dataset

Abstract

We introduce Sketchtopia, a large-scale dataset and AI framework designed to explore goal-driven, multimodal communication through asynchronous interactions in a Pictionary-inspired setup. Sketchtopia captures natural human interactions, including freehand sketches, open-ended guesses, and iconic feedback gestures, showcasing the complex dynamics of cooperative communication under constraints. It features over 20K gameplay sessions from 916 players, capturing 263K sketches, 10K erases, 56K guesses and 19.4K iconic feedbacks. We introduce multimodal foundational agents with capabilities for generative sketching, guess generation and asynchronous communication. Our dataset also includes 800 human-agent sessions for benchmarking the agents. We introduce novel metrics to characterize collaborative success, responsiveness to feedback and inter-agent asynchronous communication. Sketchtopia pushes the boundaries of multimodal AI, establishing a new benchmark for studying asynchronous, goal-oriented interactions between humans and AI agents.

Real-world communication is asynchronous, multimodal, and feedback-driven ‚Äì qualities often missing in AI. Sketchtopia bridges this gap, introducing a novel framework for natural AI communication. We present:

Large-Scale Asynchronous Sketch Dataset

Sketchtopia provides the **largest and richest dataset** for studying asynchronous multimodal communication through sketching. Featuring 20K+ gameplay sessions, 263K+ sketches, iconic feedback, and open-ended vocabulary, it's an unparalleled resource for AI research.

Foundational Asynchronous AI Agents

Meet DRAWBOT and GUESSBOT, AI agents designed to communicate naturally in asynchronous sketch-based interactions. DRAWBOT sketches responsively, while GUESSBOT guesses intelligently in real-time, demonstrating a step towards human-like AI communication.

Novel Benchmarking Metrics

We introduce metrics tailored for asynchronous multimodal AI: AAO, FRS, and MATS. These metrics evaluate not just task success, but the naturalness and responsiveness of AI communication, establishing a new benchmark for the field.

Sketches: Time-Series Visual Communication

Our dataset features 263K+ freehand sketches, captured as dynamic, time-series SVG data. This rich format allows AI to learn from the nuanced process of sketching as communication unfolds.

Guesses: Open-Ended Textual Interpretations

Sketchtopia includes 56K+ open-ended text guesses, reflecting the diverse ways humans interpret visual clues. These guesses offer insights into real-time understanding and evolving interpretations of sketches.

Example Guesses: "fruit", "idea", "communication" - See dataset for more examples.
